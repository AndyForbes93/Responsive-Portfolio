About Me

My name is Andy Forbes. I was born in Gainesville Florida in 1993. I moved to and grew up in Tampa Bay . My biggest passions in life are hockey, music, and now coding. My hockey team is the Tampa Bay Lightning, and I try to hit the ice and play hockey twice a week. I am still new to the coding world (only around 4 months of being self-taught), but I've found myself becoming more and more excited to learn about programming. I'm always eager to pick up new tools in my coding arsenal.

I am a very creative person. Its fun to inject a little of your personality into the programs you make. In my expierence so far I've found coding to be a great way to strech my more creative muscles and I hope that I can continue to grow and eventually build bigger and better things. I can't wait to see what else I'm going to design.
